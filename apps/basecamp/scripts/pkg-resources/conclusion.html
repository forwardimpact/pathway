Basecamp is installed

The scheduler is running in the background and will start automatically on login.

Next steps:

1. Edit your identity file:

vim ~/Documents/Personal/USER.md

2. Configure scheduled tasks:

vim ~/.fit/basecamp/scheduler.json

3. Check scheduler status:

fit-basecamp --status

4. Open your knowledge base interactively:

cd ~/Documents/Personal && claude

To uninstall, run: /usr/local/share/fit-basecamp/uninstall.sh
